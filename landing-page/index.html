The purpose of Immigrant News is to offer a platform for immigrants to inform and be informed about safety issues that undocumented immigrants may face.

Conceptualization

The internet and social networks such as Facebook allow news affecting a community to spread fast, through established news sources such as the New York Times, new digital news outlets such as Salon, by citizen-journalists unaffiliated with public or private institutions, and through personal testimony published in personal social media accounts. In recent years checking the accuracy of news before false claims are spread has become very difficult due to the speed at which news can be shared, and the number of people it can be shared with. Both documented and undocumented immigrants can be susceptible to false claims due to the lack of official news sources regarding federal enforcement of immigration, and due to the way information is distributed unevenly throughout the community. Immigrant News aims to offer a platform where immigrants can find, share and verify truthful information about immigration activity and related news around the country. It aims to tackle the specific problem of verifying news and stopping rumors for a community that is always seeking reliable and up to date information in a time of political uncertainty.

Gathering Requirements

We conducted a focus group interview with three individuals, self-identified as immigrants. Our interview included both structured and unstructured questions with the objective to get answers for specific doubts as well as to collect unsolicited and useful clues and advice. During the focus group, we split the process into three parts by discussing the project abstract, the project proposal, and finally the personas that we developed for the project. After discussing each part, we asked prepared questions, and asked impromptu questions.

Location

The web application will allow the user to search for news by keyword, date, and/or location

Details

The web application will display details of a story when the user selects a story from the news feed

Community Contributions

The web application will display an anonymous list of users who have contributed to a story and their respective trust rating

Add Stories

The web application will allow the user to contribute a new story to the database.

Validation

The web application will allow users to validate or invalidate a story.

Personal Additions

The web application will allow users to contribute to an existing story (contribute to the story with details or media (i.e. pictures), add comments, react, etc.)

Design + Sketches

We scheduled in-person meetings to create our sketches. All team members were in attendance and active participants during the sketch process. We went through a sketching and design process for 4 design challenges that we chose from our requirements. We will highlight the design process for one of our challenges, and the details of the rest are available here.

Design Challenge: Allow users to see the details of a story and contribute/interact with it.

The Process: For each iteration of our design challenge, we allotted ourselves 5 minutes to sketch as many ideas we had for the given challenge. After the 5 minutes, we presented our sketch ideas. Thereafter, we critiqued each otherâ€™s sketches and recorded the critiques which would help focus the design sketches for the next iteration. We followed this process for each iteration until we converged to one design sketch. The final sketch was typically drawn by one team member.
